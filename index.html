Welcome to Temperature Quilt Design

Easily map yearly temperatures to temperature ranges and colors for your temperature quilt.

A temperature quilt is a creative and colorful way to document daily temperature changes over a year (or any chosen time period). Each day, you create a small quilt / crochet block using fabrics that represent the high or low temperatures of that day. By the end of the year, youâ€™ll have a vibrant quilt that visually captures the weather patterns of your location.
